CoverSpec Studio
Welcome to CoverSpec Studio

Production-ready cover specifications made in an instant. A set of practical tools to calculate spine width, generate PDF or SVG templates, convert paper metrics, and create ISBN barcodes for book cover production. Select a tab above to get started.

Cover Templates
Limp  ·  Cover With Flaps  ·  Dust Jacket  ·  PPC

Calculates the exact dimensions for your cover artwork document — spine width, overall document width and height — based on the trim size, paper stock, and number of pages. Four binding types are supported:

  • Limp / Paperback — standard perfect-bound softcover.
  • Limp with Flaps (8pp) — softcover with folding flaps front and back.
  • Dust Jacket — printed jacket for a cased (hardback) book, with flaps and turn-ins.
  • Printed Paper Case (PPC) — printed cover wrapped directly onto hardback boards.

How to use: Select your cover type, then enter the page trim height and width, paper grammage (weight in gsm), paper volume, and the total number of pages. Contact your Printer for details of paper stocks. For jackets and PPCs also select the board thickness. Results update automatically. Two download options are available: Download PDF Template saves a to-scale vector PDF for use as a guide layer. Download SVG Template exports a native vector file (fully editable in Illustrator, Affinity Designer, InDesign) with all guides, dimensions, and section fills pre-configured — open it directly and place artwork on top. The layouts are created at the finished size — don't forget to add a 3mm bleed allowance in your document setup.

If you create a lot of covers/jackets in InDesign we highly recommend this plugin: danrodney.com/scripts/makebookjacket.html

Micron → Volume
Convert paper thickness to bulk factor

Paper volume — sometimes called the bulk factor — describes how thick a sheet of paper feels relative to its weight. It is one of the inputs needed to calculate spine width. If your paper supplier has given you a thickness in microns (µ) rather than a volume figure, use this tool to convert it.

How to use: Enter the paper thickness in microns and the grammage in gsm. The volume result appears immediately. Copy it into the Volume field in the Cover Templates tool.

ISBN Barcode
Generate a print-ready EAN-13 barcode PDF

Generates a print-quality EAN-13 ISBN barcode as a vector PDF sized to your exact requirements. The barcode is drawn as native vector shapes — not a bitmap image — so it will print cleanly at any size without pixelation. An optional EAN-5 five-digit price supplement can be added alongside the main barcode.

How to use: Enter your ISBN-13 number (hyphens are stripped automatically). Optionally enter a 5-digit add-on supplement. Set your preferred width and height in millimetres. A preview updates as you type. Click Download PDF Barcode and place the PDF into your cover artwork.
